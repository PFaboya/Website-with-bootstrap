Classes

Slayer

Armed with a huge sword these melee combatant deal heavy damage with combo attacks

Warrior

Armed with dual sword these melee combatant deal damage with lightning fast combo attacks

Berserker

weilding a great-axe they deal damage with charged up attacks

Archer

These ranged damage dealers charge up their attacks from a distance

Sorcerer

Use fire and ice spells to nuke them enemies

Gunner

Weilding an arc-cannon and accompanied by robots they can deal damage and heal themselves from a distance. only high elf and castanic females may pick this class

Valkyrie

Armed with a spear cause continuous damage with their combos only castanic females may be this class

Ninja

Have a giant shuriken they use a combination of skills to deal damage only elins can be this class

Reaper

Use they chain-hooks to deal large aoe damage only elins can be this class

Priest

Heal, buff and shield allies while debuffing the enemy

Mystic

Heal and buff allies like priests but can also summon thralls to attack and heal themselves

Lancer

Tanks that are armed with a shield and lance, can buff allies and block attacks with their shield

Brawler

Tanks armed with giant guantlets that do not buff allies but deal more damage, they block while using specific skill combos
